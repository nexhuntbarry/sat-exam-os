# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: 2026-04-20**

This Privacy Policy describes how SAT Exam OS ("the Platform") collects, uses, and protects personal information.

## 1. Data We Collect

### Student Data
- Full name and display name
- Email address
- Grade, school, campus, class group
- Parent/guardian name, email, and phone number
- Target SAT score and current level
- Test responses, scores, and performance history

### Teacher / Admin Data
- Name and email address
- Role and assigned classes

### Usage Data
- Login timestamps
- Test session activity (time spent, answers submitted)

## 2. How We Use Data

Data is used to:
- Provide the test management and analytics service
- Allow teachers to monitor student progress
- Enable administrators to manage their tutoring center

We do **not** sell personal data to third parties.

## 3. Minors (Under 18)

We recognize the importance of protecting children's privacy. Tutoring centers using this Platform are responsible for:
- Obtaining verifiable parental consent before registering students under 18
- Ensuring parent contact information is accurate and current
- Complying with applicable children's privacy laws (e.g., COPPA in the US, PDPA in Taiwan)

## 4. Data Hosting and Security

Data is stored on:
- **Supabase** — PostgreSQL database with row-level security capabilities
- **Vercel** — Application hosting and deployment infrastructure
- **Vercel Blob** — PDF file storage

We implement reasonable technical safeguards including HTTPS, access controls, and service-role key management.

## 5. Data Retention

Student data is retained as long as the tutoring center account is active. Upon account termination, data may be deleted upon request within 30 days.

## 6. Your Rights

Users (or parents/guardians of minors) may request:
- Access to their personal data
- Correction of inaccurate data
- Deletion of their data

Contact your tutoring center administrator to exercise these rights.

## 7. Jurisdiction

This Platform is operated for tutoring centers in **Taiwan (R.O.C.)** and the **United States**. We comply with applicable privacy regulations in these jurisdictions.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the Platform after updates constitutes acceptance.

## 9. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact the administrator at your tutoring center.
